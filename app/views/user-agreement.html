Step 3 of 8 Welcome to the analyst sandbox

To use the analyst sandbox, please review and agree to this user agreement

  • I have read the Data science ethical framework and agree that when working in the sandbox, I will be aware of its six key principles:
    • Start with clear user need and public benefit.
    • Use data and tools which have the minimum intrusion necessary
    • Create robust data science models
    • Be alert to public perceptions
    • Be as open and accountable as possible
    • Keep data secure
  • I understand my responsibilities under the UK Data Protection Act 1998 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and will act accordingly
  • I will only use publicly available open data, or government data classified as OFFICIAL - and will take appropriate measures to ensure that sensitive data is only shared on a need-to-know basis. I will not use data classified as SECRET or TOP SECRET
  • If I have any questions about the ethics or security of my work in the sandbox, I will stop and ask for help
